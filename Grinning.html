Grinning Face

😀

The grinning face with smiling eyes emoji expresses joy, happiness, cheerfulness, or excitement.
It might also be used to show an ironic attempt at positivity when things are grim.

Grinning Face emoji is a happy-looking smiley, showing Teeth — this is one of the most popular and used emojis of all. It looks so joyful and kind that it is almost never used in sarcastic or ironic meanings — only in the context of laughing, rejoicing, and being excited at something like the upcoming holiday. Often, Grinning Face used as a reply to a joke someone liked; and often it serves as a sign of strong appreciation of someone’s actions or achievements, as well as a sign of feeling happy for any reason imaginable, Loving life, and staying positive.
